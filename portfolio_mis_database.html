MIS Databases Elysian Fly Company

Aug - Dec 2020

At the beginning of this project, we met with the client after having read up on the company to ask questions and gather information that would assist us in creating this database. Once all of our questions were answered, we began with the construction of the ERD, using both the revenue and expenditure cycles. Once this step was completed, we began the process of Normalization. During the Normalization process, we sought to convert the Entity Relationship Diagram to Third Normal Form in order to ensure that all tables within the database were atomic and also to eliminate data redundancies.

When building the database using the VMWare Enterprise Systems client as well as Microsoft SQL Server Management Studio to build a database one table at a time that both functions properly and generates the desired data, provided by generatedata.com, and does so quickly and efficiently which can be further achieved through the process of denormalization. Once we could ensure that the provided SQL Queries were both correctly answered and provided the proper results, we did the same with the three additional queries that our group came up with.

At the conclusion of this project, the project management portion revealed a total of 189 hours and 20 minutes amounting to a total project cost of $4,733. Broken down further, Milestone One had a combined work time of 51 hours and 30 minutes and a cost of $1,288, Milestone Two had a combined work time of 17 and a cost of $425, Milestone Three had a combined work time of 42 hours and 40 minutes and a cost of $1,067, and Final Submission had a combined work time of 78 hours and 10 minutes with a cost of $1,954.
