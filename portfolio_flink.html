Flink Introduction

Flink is a Berlin-based startup that sells food and grocery items at supermarket prices and has the goal to deliver them under 10 minutes. Currently, Flink is operating in more than 60 cities across four countries: Germany, France, Netherlands, and Austria. Their competitors include other delivery services like Gorillaz, Deliveroo, and others. Flink's estimated value is currently around $5 billion accordingn to Dealroom.co.

How Does Flink Operate?

The company operates dark warehouses and stores in the cities they are currently operating in. A dedicated staff of warerhouse pickers collects the items ordered from the app, and hands them over to drivers who will deliver them. The drivers are actually employees by Flink on a full-time basis. Flink provides them e-bikes to make the deliveries, which is a big contrast to other services like Deliveroo or Instacart, which only hire drivers on a contractual basis.
